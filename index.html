Privacy Policy for Furqan

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Furqan, a Quran app designed to help users read, study, and engage with the Holy Quran. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any data you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • ✔ What information we collect
  • ✔ How we use your data
  • ✔ Your privacy choices

2. Information We Collect

A. User-Provided Information

  • 📌 Bookmarks: Saved verses for quick access
  • 📖 Last Read Position: Helping you continue where you left off
  • 🔤 Font Settings: Personalization for better readability

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • 📍 Location Data: To provide accurate prayer times (you may enter location manually instead)
  • 📱 Device Information: Basic details to improve functionality & crash reporting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • ✔ Provide Quran reading, prayer times, and customization features
  • ✔ Improve the app based on anonymous usage data
  • ✔ Remember your preferences for a seamless experience

4. Data Storage & Security

🔒 Your data is stored securely on your device.

🚫 We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted external services for specific features:

  • 🕌 Prayer Times API (Aladhan.com) – Uses location data for accurate prayer times
  • 🌍 OpenStreetMap – Converts coordinates into city names
  • 🎵 Audio Recitation Services – Streams Quranic recitations from EveryAyah.com or QuranicAudio.com

6. Your Choices

  • 🔹 Location Services: Disable location permissions anytime in your device settings
  • 🔹 Storage Permissions: App may request storage access for offline audio functionality (optional)
  • 🔹 Data Deletion: Clear app data in device settings whenever needed

7. Children's Privacy

🚸 Our app does not target children under 13.

🔍 If personal data is collected from a child under 13, we delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

📢 We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here, along with an updated Effective Date.
